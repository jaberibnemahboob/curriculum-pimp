Kea life

The KEA school is providing a whole studying environment and not only by its pedagogia but also by all the features that they are proposing to their student

Kea Masterclasses

Only from the second semester, you will be able to participate to the Masterclasses provided by sometimes some KEA students, sometimes some extra guest sharing specific skills.

Kea Library

The Kea Library is free to use for each student which are part of the Kea School, there you can borrow books, participate to the local event life, entertain yourself with the Kea resources.

Friday Bars

Each friday in every building of the Kea institution, you have a Friday Bar event, which is a comfort and social way to enjoy the end of your week and the beginning of the week-end.

Kea Cantine

The Kea cantine is a nice place made for the students used by the students where you can eat during eating time then meet and work in the rest of the time.
